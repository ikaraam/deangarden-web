# Deangarden | Landscape Intelligence

Connecting AI, robotics and digital twins to observe, understand and steward natural environments.

## Vision

Deangarden explores persistent landscape intelligence platforms that combine robotics, remote sensing, AI and digital twins to create continuously updated representations of natural environments.

Our aim is to help observe, understand and steward landscapes through biodiversity monitoring, carbon intelligence and resilience to environmental change.

## Current Focus

- Landscape Intelligence
- Robotics
- Remote Sensing
- Digital Twins
- Environmental Monitoring
- Biodiversity
- Woodland Stewardship
